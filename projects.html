The Scheduler

This is a simplified version of a scheduling tool that I created and use on a weekly basis. Currently in beta, it is also being tested by multiple groups for handling scheduling at their respective locations as well. In a nutshell, it is a Google Workspace application that is used for the development, collaboration, management, and distribution of schedules for any business. It also handles tracking the amount of workers at any given time, saving and marking request-off dates submitted by associates, placing associates in different groups, schedule appearance customizations, and more. To avoid the use of any permissions scopes, this link leads to a simplified version that lacks any of the server functionality, such as saving or loading schedules and sending emails.

  • Skills Displayed:
  • Google Apps Script Engine using CLASP
  • Vanilla JavaScript/HTML/CSS
  • Workflow Simplification and Automation
