About Me

Hello, and thank you for taking the time to check out my personal website! I have a passion for learning, which not only includes just Web Development. I am a novice-level German language enthusiast, and have been learning one-on-one with my tutor since early April of 2020.

Before I discovered my passion for Web Development, I worked in management of various operations. At age 21, I became the General Manager of a fast food restaurant, a position I held for almost three years before deciding it was not sustainable. Since then, I have gained experience in retail, and now I am currently a supervisor in the gaming industry.

I have always been a "nerd" at heart, but after discovering my passion for Web Development, my life changed. I have since re-enrolled in school in pursuit of a related degree, which I am finishing in the Spring of 2021. When it comes to Web Development, I have experience in the following categories:

  • Front-end using HTML5, CSS and vanilla JavaScript
  • ReactJS
  • Back-end using NodeJS
  • Template Engines
  • Web Scraping
  • Data Analysis/Visualization

I have discovered that I am at my best when I am consistently learning and progressing myself. At this point, I don't see any other way to live.
